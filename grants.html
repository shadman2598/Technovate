We welcome inquiries from grant programs, government agencies, accelerators, and institutional partners.

Why fund Technovate

Technovate addresses real needs across multiple sectors with technology that is understandable, auditable, and designed for public benefit. Our solutions are structured for institutional adoption from day one — not retrofitted after the fact.

We operate with defined milestones, clear deliverables, and transparent reporting. Funders know exactly what their investment supports, when milestones will be met, and how outcomes are measured.

Our cross-sector approach means a single technology investment creates impact across health, finance, education, enterprise, and community development. This multiplier effect is rare and valuable for funders seeking broad economic and social returns.

We're building Canadian technology capacity in AI and software — creating jobs, developing exportable IP, and establishing frameworks that other organizations can adopt and build upon.
